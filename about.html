About the author

NEVER IN SEASON, ALWAYS ON TIME

R. Adriel Vasquez is a Texas native born in 1982. He has never had a southern accent, and knows 20 Spanish words—14 of which are numbers. Adriel went to Prague in 2006 where he planned to stay for a year. He left 15 years later.

His experience in Central Europe is exaggerated somewhat in the self-published novel 5 Cigarettes::. A Book About Love, Music, Sex, Death, and Rebith, available from this website.

Adriel currently lives in Amsterdam, where he makes a living as a technical writer, and works on his next novel—which will be either a love story in Hell, or about parties in Prague c. 2017-19. Same difference.

WHAT THE AUTHOR IS ABOUT

On his blog LO-RES/HI-LIFE, Adriel shares anecdotes (and antidotes) against the influence of seriousness, or field notes for wellness under dystopia.

Well acquainted with neurosis, abandonment and attachment issues, substance abuse and depression—Adriel dedicates his experience, research, and skills to helping anyone feeling lost, stuck, unrelatable, alone, and out of touch.

LO-RES/HI-LIFE is about learning to calm the fuck down, and live an authentic and creative life that generates and serves meaning first and foremost to yourself.

He has also collected some of his favorite photos over the years and published a book, which you can check out here.

Adriel also makes fucked up music and visual art as Aislenyne.
