5 Cigarettes::.

SEX IS COOL, BUT DID YOU EVER QUIT SCHOOL TO GET MARRIED AND MOVE TO PRAGUE FOR A YEAR, THEN DIVORCE AND LEAVE 15 YEARS, 3 BANDS, 7 FLATS, 2 DEGREES, 3 RELATIONSHIPS, 1 SHORT FILM, AND 1 PSYCHOTIC BREAK LATER?

Part memoir, part journaling device. Humorous, lyrical, upsetting and heart-breaking. 5 Cigarettes::. is my gift back to the Central European city, people—and to anyone lost who wants to be found—for teaching me to accept that my life needed saving.

Coming fall 2024. E-book and physical copies to order.

Meanwhile, listen to the playlist.

ABOUT THE BOOK

As the title and subtitle suggest, 5 Cigarettes::. is a collection of experiences under the categories Love, Music, Sex, Death, and Rebirth. Events take place between 2006 and 2021.
